About me

My love for web design began the summer of 2003 when my best friend introduced me to Neopets. We spent every night and weekend dedicated to learning HTML & CSS to make the *~coolest~* guild and profile layouts.

Fast forward to 2020, I decided to change careers after 5 years as an elementary school counselor. My husband started working from home as a web developer so now we are a programming duo. ;)

Looking back, I see now that in every job I have had, I have always been gravitating towards aspects of creating practical, efficient solutions, as well as incorporating design elements into my systems and routines.

When I was an elementary school counselor, one of the first big projects I did was create my own data collection system that was easy for other stakeholders to view graphically and access easily. I also loved creating digital infographics for parents as a way to connect with them and also express my creativity.

Now that I'm a mother of 4, it's a dream to be able to do what I love from home and maximize time with my family. In my free time, I love cooking, DIY projects, sewing, painting, gardening, and reading fantasy. Thank you for taking the time to learn about me! ♥
